# ✅ **Understanding Your Output File (Easy Explanation)**

Your final output file contains **MSL coverage details** for the month.  
Each column has a clear purpose.

Below is the meaning of each important column:

***

## 🟦 **1. Account: Customer Code**

*   This is the **unique doctor code**.
*   It identifies each doctor in your ABM report.

***

## 🟦 **2. MSL Dr**

*   **TRUE** → This doctor belongs to the **MSL universe** (meaning MSL is responsible for this doctor).
*   **FALSE** → Not part of MSL universe.

***

## 🟦 **3. Visited This Month**

*   **TRUE** → Doctor was **visited by MSL during the selected month** (Jan 2026 in your script).
*   **FALSE** → No visit done in that month.

***

## 🟦 **4. Doctors list (MSL doctors)**

*   Total **number of MSL doctors in the hierarchy file**.
*   Same number repeats on each row as a KPI indicator.

***

## 🟦 **5. Doctors visit in that month (DCR report)**

*   How many MSL doctors were actually **visited** in that month.
*   Also repeated on each row for reference.

***

## 🟦 **6. Coverage %**

*   Formula:
        (Visited MSL Doctors / Total MSL Doctors) × 100
*   Shows **how much of the MSL universe was covered**.

***
